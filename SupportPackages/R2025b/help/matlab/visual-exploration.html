Data Exploration

Explore data with visualizations or sonification

Analyze and interpret your data by creating data visualizations to view your data as a chart or by using data sonification to convert your data to sound. For example, you can create line plots, bubble charts, or histograms of your data to analyze trends, identify patterns, or gain other insights.
