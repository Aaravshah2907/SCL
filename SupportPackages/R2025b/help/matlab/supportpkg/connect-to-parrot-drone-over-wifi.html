Connect to Parrot Drone over Wi-Fi

Introduction

To connect your computer to your Parrot® Drone over Wi-Fi®, see:

  • Parrot Mambo Drone

  • Parrot Bebop2 Drone

Connect to Parrot Mambo Drone Over Wi-Fi

To connect your computer to the Parrot Mambo drone Wi-Fi, follow these instructions:

  1. Insert the battery and power on the Parrot Mambo FPV drone.

  2. Wait for the LEDs in the camera to stabilize.

  3. Connect to the drone's Wi-Fi network as shown.

Connect to Parrot Bebop 2 Drone Over Wi-Fi

To connect your computer to the Parrot Bebop 2 drone Wi-Fi, follow these instructions:

  1. Insert the battery and power on the Parrot Bebop 2 drone.

  2. Connect to the drone's Wi-Fi network as shown.
