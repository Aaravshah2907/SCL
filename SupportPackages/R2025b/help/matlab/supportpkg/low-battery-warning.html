Troubleshooting Low Battery Warning

Description

The low-battery warning indicates that the drone battery must be replaced. The drone performance may be reduced under low-battery conditions and it is not advised to perform advanced maneuvers like flips in such conditions.

The drone will force land and ignore further takeoff commands if the flight is continued post the warning message.

Action

  1. Land the drone

  2. Turn off the drone

  3. Replace drone batteries

  4. Connect computer to drone’s Wi-Fi® network

  5. Clear the existing ryze object and create a new one
