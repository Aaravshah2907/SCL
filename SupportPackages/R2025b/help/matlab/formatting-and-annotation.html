Labels and Styling

Choose colors, annotations, and themes; modify axes; control plot interactions

Customize the appearance of your plots to convey additional information or enhance the display of the data. For example, you can add titles and labels, change the axis limits, or customize data tips. You can also combine multiple sets of data in one figure, either by showing all the data together in one plot or by displaying a tiling of plots in one figure.
