Hardware and Network Communication

Connect to hardware, such as serial port devices, Arduino boards, and webcams

MathWorks® provides built-in support for some hardware, including devices connected by serial port, Bluetooth®, and TCP/IP. For other hardware, such as Arduino® boards or webcams, you can download and install support packages. For details on available support packages across all MathWorks products, see the Hardware Support Catalog.
