Interactions, Camera Views, and Lighting

Control interactions, cameras, lighting, and transparency

Customize plot interactions or the appearance of a scene. For example, you can change the transparency and color shading of plotted objects. In 3-D scenes, you can adjust the lighting, rotate the scene, zoom in or out, and change the camera position.
