Trace Information

The digital thread can analyze your project, identify and trace relationships between artifacts, and serve as a central source of traceability information for several MathWorks® apps and tools.

Cache trace information.  

The digital thread can analyze artifacts and their relationships more efficiently and reduce analysis time by caching certain traceability information inside project artifacts, like models. In the Digital Thread Settings dialog box, select Cache trace information.

The next time that you save the project artifact, the digital thread caches the associated trace information inside the artifact.
